The Big 3 Anime of all time.

By Titus Dithin T Titus

1. One Piece.

Certainly! **One Piece** is an epic anime that follows the adventures of **Monkey D. Luffy** and his spirited crew, known as the **Straw Hat Pirates**. Their quest leads them across the treacherous **Grand Line**, driven by a singular ambition: to lay claim to the legendary and mysterious treasure known as the "One Piece".
Luffy's ultimate goal?
To become the next
**King of the Pirates**!

2. Naruto.

Naruto follows the adventures of Naruto Uzumaki, a mischievous and determined ninja-in-training.
Orphaned at birth, Naruto seeks recognition and dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village.
The series unfolds in different time periods, including
Part I, where Naruto joins Team 7 and faces challenges like the Chūnin Exams and the Konoha Crush. Later,
in Part II, he confronts his friend Sasuke Uchiha and embarks on various missions.
The story weaves action, comedy, and themes of friendship and perseverance.

3. Bleach.

Bleach is an anime series that aired from October 2004 to March 2012, spanning 366 episodes.
The story follows Ichigo Kurosaki, a high school student with the ability to see ghosts.
After obtaining powers from a Soul Reaper named Rukia Kuchiki, Ichigo becomes a Soul Reaper himself—a death personification similar to the Grim Reaper.
The series delves into the world of Shinigami (Soul Reapers) and their conflicts with Arrancar and Quincy.
